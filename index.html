About

Mayowa Dorcas victor (born 19 December), professionally known as YOWA is a Nigerian singer and songwriter single-handedly redefining the Nigerian music scene with her Afro RnB sound.

Yowa brings an urban sound that is undeniably unique, embodying a fusion of Afro beats, soul and RnB genres.
